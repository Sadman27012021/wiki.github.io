Advice of Lukman

10 advices are:

1. Ascribe no partner to Allah. Surely ascribing partners (to Him) is a grievous iniquity.

2. Even if it be the weight of a grain of mustard-seed, even though it be in a rock, or in the heaven or in the earth, Allah will bring it forth. Surely Allah is Knower of subtilities, Aware .

3. Stablish prayer.

4. Enjoin what is right.

5. Forbid what is wrong.

6. Be patient over what befalls you.

7. And turn not thy face away from people in contempt.

8. Do not walk through the earth exultantly. Surely Allah loves not any self-conceited boaster.

9. Be moderate in your pace.

10. Lower your voice.
